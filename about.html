About · Tools · CV

About

I am a France-based 3D Artist with a background in cinematography and directing photography. My work focuses on dark fantasy, cosmic horror and industrial environments, always with a strong sense of cinematic composition.

What I do

  • Characters and creatures for dark fantasy and horror worlds.
  • Vehicles and props with a grounded, industrial feel.
  • Abyssal and historical environments with strong mood and lighting.
  • Keyframes and short sequences for trailers, reels and short films.

How I work

  • Start from a clear narrative intention: who we follow, what moment, what emotion.
  • Block out composition and lighting first, then detail where it matters most on screen.
  • Use non-destructive texturing with layered masks, grime and storytelling in the surfaces.
  • Keep cinematic lighting and grading coherent across shots and sequences.

Tools · Pipeline · Gear

Core tools

Blender · ZBrush · Substance Painter · Photoshop · DaVinci Resolve · Marvelous Designer · LiquiGen · Cascadeur · Gaea 2.0 · RealityScan (latest versions).

Pipeline

  • Blender / ZBrush – sculpt & hard surface modeling, retopology, UVs, rigging.
  • Substance Painter – physically based texturing with layered masks and fine surface details.
  • Blender · Cycles – lookdev & final rendering with cinematic lighting.
  • DaVinci Resolve – compositing, grading and final edit of the reel or short film.

Inspirations

My main reference is Ian Hubert, for the way he blends gritty realism, storytelling and insanely efficient workflows. I also draw inspiration from artists such as:

  • Jama Jurabaev – cinematic concept art & mood-driven environments.
  • Marek Denko – photoreal urban / industrial storytelling.
  • Raphaël Lacoste – large-scale worlds and atmospheric vistas.
  • Paul Chadeisson – heavy industry, sci-fi architecture and scale.
  • Pascal Blanche – stylised yet dark, myth-infused worlds.
  • Vitaly Bulgarov – detailed hard-surface design and mechs.
